Planet 4: Mars

Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun in our Solar System. It is the second-smallest planet (only larger than Mercury) It is named after the Roman God of War. The iron oxide on the planet makes it look red, so it is often called 'Red Planet'. Mars is a rocky planet with a thin atmosphere. On the top & bottom, it has polar-like features but in the middle it is more like a desert.
