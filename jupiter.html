Planet 5: Jupiter

Jupiter is the fifth planet in the Solar System. It is also the largest planet. All the planets before were rocky planets , but Jupiter is the first gas planet. Jupiter's mass is 2.5x more than all the other planets' mass combined! It is named after the Roman god Jupiter , the god of lightning, and the skies. It is composed of 75% hydrogen and 25% helium. There is also a huge, turbulent storm on Jupiter called the "Great Red Spot". It is a reddish color and was first discovered in 17th Century by telescope.
