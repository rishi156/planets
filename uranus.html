Planet 7: Uranus

Uranus is the seventh planet in the Solar System. It is named after the Greek god of the Sky! Uranus and Neptune share many similiraties, compared to other gas planets like Jupiter and Saturn; so they grouped together by scientists. For this reason, scientists often classify Uranus and Neptune as "ice giants" to distinguish them from the other giant planets. Speaking of giants, Uranus is the third gas giant, and first ice giant.
