Planet 2: Venus

Venus is the second planet from the Sun. It is named after the Roman goddess of love. Since Venus is so close to the Sun, it can easily reflect light and cast shadows on the Earth. Thus, Venus can sometimes be visible to us before sunrise or after sunset. Also, Venus (along with Uranus) is the only planet that rotates counter-clockwise on its axis. Venus orbits the Sun in almost every 225 earthdays. And, its rotation period is 243 earthdays. Venus is the only other planet (except Mercury) that doesn't have a natural satellite/ a moon.
