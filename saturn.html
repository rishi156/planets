Planet 6: Saturn

Saturn is the sixth planet in the solar system and the second-largest (after Jupiter.) It is also the second gas planet. Its density is 1/8th of Earth (because it is made of gas), but it's so large that it is actually 95x more massive! Saturn is named after the Roman god of wealth and agriculture. The planet is very famous because of its huge rings!
