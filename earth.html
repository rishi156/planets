Planet 3: Earth

Earth is the third planet from the Sun. It is a very special planet because it is the only one which can support life. Earth is in the correct zone of space to support life, as it is not too close (and too hot) or too far away (and too cold). Earth supports life with 3 main portions - lithosphere (land) , hydrosphere (water) and atmosphere (air). Earth has 2 main parts - Land (continents) and Water (oceans); it is 71% water and 29% land. There are 7 continents on Earth, which are - Asia, Africa, North America, South America, Antarctica, Europe and Australia. Also, there are 5 oceans on Earth which are - Pacific, Atlantic, Indian, Arctic and Southern Oceans.

Did you know?

Many people think the Earth is a sphere, but actually it's not! It's more like a flattened sphere (called oblate spheroid). It is thin at the top & bottom and fat in the middle!
