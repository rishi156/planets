Planet 1: Mercury

Mercury is the first (and smallest) planet in the Solar System, and it is the closest to the sun. It is named after the Roman god Mercurius, who is the connection between humans and gods. Like Venus, Mercury is so close to the Sun that, we can see it after Sunset and before Sunrise. When we see it, it will appear blurry and not as clear as Venus. Also, Mercury is one of the only planets which doesn't have a natural satellite (a moon.) One of the probes which orbited Mercury was made by NASA and it was called the MESSENGER.
